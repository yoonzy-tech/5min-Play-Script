五分鐘話劇劇本

5-min Drama Script

人物 (Cast)

●                 旁白 (Narrator)

●                 媽祖／林默 (Mazu / Lin Moniang)

●                 媽祖的媽媽 (Mazu’s mother)

●                 媽祖的爸爸 (Mazu’s father)

●                 媽祖的哥哥 (Mazu’s brother)

●                 Eric 台灣學生 (Taiwanese student)

●                 Tiffany 台灣學生 (Taiwanese student)

●                 Manon 法國學生 (French student)

●                 Amber 越南學生 (Vietnamese student)

●                 攤販老闆 (Vendor)

 

劇本 (Script)

第一幕(Scene One)：誰是媽祖 Who’s Mazu?

旁白 Narrator：Manon 和 Amber 是來台灣學習華語的國際學生，每次他們去學校的路上都會經過一間媽祖廟，他們想了解台灣寺廟敬拜文化。

(Manon and Amber are international students who came to Taiwan to study Chinese. Every time they go to school, they pass by a Mazu temple. They want to learn about Taiwanese temple worship culture.)

 

旁白 Narrator：一個晴朗的下午， 他們找了班上的台灣學生 Andrew 跟 Tiffany 一起去參觀，希望他們可以帶他們了解台灣的文化。

(One sunny afternoon, they asked Andrew and Tiffany, the Taiwanese students in their class, to visit together, hoping that they could help them understand Taiwan’s culture.)

 

在寺廟前 (In front of the temple)

 

Manon：終於，我們來了！那這座寺廟裡的神是什麼呢？我聽說臺灣的每座寺廟都有不同的神，儘管寺廟裡還有很多其他的神明。

(Finally, we are here. So, what’s the god featuring in this temple? I heard that each temple in Taiwan features a different god, even though there are also many other gods sitting in the temple.)

 

Eric：你說得對。就像在中國文化中一樣，有許多神，不像基督教或穆斯林，他們相信只有一個真神。我相信我們更像希臘文化，在希臘文化中，他們有很多神，而每個神都有自己的故事和個性。

(You are right on that. As in Chinese culture, there are many gods, unlike Christian or Muslim, which believe there’s only one true god. I believe we’re more like Greek culture, in which they have many gods and each god has its own story and personality.)

 

Tiffany：這座寺廟祭拜臺灣最著名的女神媽祖，她被崇拜為海水女神。

(This temple worships the goddess Mazu(媽祖). She is worshiped as the sea and water goddess.)

Amber：為什麼呢？是因為臺灣是一個四面環海的島嶼嗎？

(Why? Is it because Taiwan is an island surrounded by the sea?)

 

Eric：是的，這也是她成為臺灣重要神的原因之一。

(Yes, that is one of the reasons that makes her become the important god in Taiwan.)

 

Manon：媽祖是真人，對吧？她最初不是神明。

(Mazu was a real person, right? She wasn’t a god initially.)

 

Eric：是的，她一開始不是。這就得從歷史開始說起。媽祖不是她的真名。她的名字叫林默娘。她的名字如果按字面意思翻譯“沉默的少女”。據說媽祖直到一個多月大才哭過。

(Yes, she wasn’t a god in the beginning. This is where history comes in. Mazu isn’t her real and first name. Her name was Lin Moniang (林默娘). Her name, if translated literally, is ‘silent girl’ or ‘silent maiden’. It is said that Mazu never cried until she was over a month old.)

第二幕(Scene Two)：媽祖的由來故事 The Story of Mazu

媽媽抱著女嬰 (The mother was holding the baby girl)

 

媽祖的媽媽 Mazu’s mother：哎呦，這孩子怎麼都不會哭的呢？(Oh, why doesn’t this child cry at all?)

 

旁白 Narrator：媽祖從出生到滿月都沒有哭過，住的地方靠近大海居民都靠抓魚為生，常常聽到出海抓魚的鄰居出意外的消息，想幫助他們，但是什麼也做不了，而感到傷心難過。

(Mazu, from birth until the full moon, never shed a tear. He lived in a coastal area where residents made their living by fishing. He often heard news of neighbors encountering accidents while fishing at sea. He wanted to help them, but felt powerless and saddened.)

 

旁白 Narrator：某天，媽祖發現自己有預知未來的能力...

(One day, the goddess Matsu discovered her own ability to foresee the future…)

 

媽祖看著窗外的海發呆，她的母親在廚房煮飯

(Mazu stared at the sea outside the window in a daze, while her mother was cooking in the kitchen)

 

媽祖的媽媽 Mazu’s mother：默娘，你在看什麼呢？

(Moniang, what are you looking at?)

 

媽祖 Mazu： 哦，沒什麼，媽媽，我只是在思考一些事情，感覺下午天氣會變差，會有大風雨，有種不好的預感。

(Oh, it’s nothing, Mom. I’m just thinking about some things. I have a feeling that the weather will worsen in the afternoon, and there will be heavy wind and rain. I feel something bad is going to happen.)

 

媽祖的父親和哥哥在船上捕魚。突然，烏雲密布，大風卷起巨浪

(Mazu’s father and brother were fishing on the boat. Suddenly, dark clouds gathered and strong winds rolled up huge waves)

 

媽祖的爸爸 Mazu’s father： 這風和雨太強了，我們得趕快回去！

(The wind and rain are too strong. We need to go back quickly!)

 

媽祖的哥哥 Mazu’s brother： 爸爸，船要沉了！

(Dad, the boat is going to sink!)

 

此時，在家中的媽祖，感覺到爸爸跟哥哥遇到了危險 (At this time, Mazu, who was at home, felt that her father and brother were in danger)

 

媽祖 Mazu： 感覺怪怪的，是不是爸爸跟哥哥有危險了？我要去幫助他們！(It feels weird. Are Dad and brother in danger? I need to help them!)

 

媽祖陷入深深的恍惚之中，她的靈魂移動到父親和哥哥的船上

(Mazu fell into a deep trance, and her soul moved to her father and brother's boat)

 

媽祖 Mazu： 爸爸，哥哥，堅持住！我看到了一切。我會幫助你們上岸。(Dad, brother, hang in there! I see everything. I will help you guys get on the shore.)

 

家中母親焦急地等待著父親和哥哥們的歸來，看到眼神奇怪的媽祖。

(The mother at home was anxiously waiting for the return of her father and brothers, and saw Mazu with strange eyes.)

 

媽祖的媽媽 Mazu’s mother： 默娘，你怎麼了？你的眼神好奇怪。(Moniang, what happened? You look strange.)

 

媽祖流了很多汗，一直哭 (Mazu sweated a lot and kept crying)

 

媽祖 Mazu：媽媽我救到了爸爸，但是，我沒抓好哥哥...

(Mom, I saved Dad, but…I couldn’t catch my brother.)

 

媽祖的媽媽：什麼？你在說什麼？

(What? What are you talking about?)

 

旁白 Narrator：不久鄰居傳說，林家在海上發生危險，爸爸平安但兒子沒有活下來...從此之後林默的預知能力被大家知道，鄰居也改口叫她媽祖，相信他會守護捕魚人的平安。

(Soon the neighbors heard that the Lin family was in danger at sea. The father was safe but the son did not survive... From then on, Lin Mo's precognitive ability became known to everyone, and the neighbors also changed their name to Mazu, believing that he would protect the safety of fishermen.)

 

第三幕 (Scene Three)：夜市美食 Night Market Gourmet

旁白 Narrator：參觀完媽祖廟後，已經是晚上了，Manon, Amber, Eric, Tiffany 來到了寺廟附近很熱鬧的夜市吃晚餐。

(After visiting the Mazu temple, the sky is already dark. Manon, Amber, Eric, and Tiffany went to the lively night market near the temple for dinner.)

 

Eric, Tiffany, Manon, Amber 走在夜市

(Eric, Tiffany, Manon, Amber were walking in the night market.)

Eric：好的，我們來到了臺北有名的夜市之一，寧夏夜市。

(So, here we are at one of the famous night markets in Taipei, Ningxia Night Market.)

 

Amber：這裡有很多人。我等不及開始我的美食體驗。

(Lots of people here. I cannot wait to start my foodie experience.)

 

Manon：在夜市上，我們必吃哪些食物？我知道臭豆腐是我們必須嘗試的東西哈哈哈，儘管我的另一個德國朋友說它聞起來真的像垃圾。

(What are the foods that we must-try in the night market? I know stinky tofu is something that we must try hahaha, although my other German friend said it really smells like trash.)

 

Eric：當然可以，你真的要嘗嘗這裡的臭豆腐。已經營業了20多年了。它從我小時候就在這裡。

(Absolutely, you really have to try the stinky tofu here. The stinky tofu stand has been open for more than 20 years. It has been here since I was little.)

Tiffany：另一種有趣的食物是臺灣版的西式漢堡。它被稱為「刈包」。它通常包裹豬肉、一些酸捲心菜、糖、花生粉和香菜。

(Another food that you’d find interesting is the Taiwanese version of Western burger. It’s called Gua Bao (刈包). It usually wraps pork, some sour cabbage, sugar, peanut powder, and coriander.)

 

Amber：噢，從來沒聽說過。這看起來確實像漢堡，但也像錢包？

(Oh, never heard of that. That does look like a burger, but also like a wallet?)

 

Tiffany：是的，確實如此，而且背後有一個故事。據說，刈包是由一個士兵發明的，他想吃中國麵包(饅頭)，並用他的刀切了麵包。

(Yes, it does and it has a story behind it. Allegedly, Gua Bao was invented by a soldier, who wants to eat Chinese bun (饅頭) and used his knife cut the bun.)

 

Tiffany：由於切好的饅頭的形狀看起來像錢包，因此它被視為財富的象徵。臺灣人通常在農曆末年吃刈包，以祈求新的一年好運。

(As the shape of the cut bun looks like a wallet or a purse, it’s been seen as the representation of fortune. Taiwanese usually have Gua Bao during the end of the lunar year to wish for great fortune in the new year.)

 

Manon: 哦，這讓我想起了“Banh Mi”！是之前在法國很受歡迎的小吃，但它來自越南。

(Oh, this reminds me of “Banh Mi”! It’s a really popular snack in France before, but it’s from Vietnam.)

 

Amber: 太酷了！我都不知道法國人會喜歡。 跟刈包不一樣的是 “Banh Mi” 是用法國麵包，我們裡面也會放一些肉、蔬菜和香草。

(Unlike Gua Bao, "Banh Mi" is made with a French baguette, and inside, we also put some meats, vegetables, and herbs.)

 

Manon: 我們也會在裡面加鵝肝醬！

(We also add pâté in it!)

 

Eric：超酷的耶！下次帶我們去試試！走吧，我們去點刈包。

(That's super cool! Next time, take us to try it! Come on, let's go order some Gua Bao.)

 

第四幕 (Scene Four)：點餐實用對話 Ordering Foods

來到了刈包小吃攤，老闆忙著出餐

(Arrived at the Gua Bao stand. The boss was busy serving the dishes to the customers)

 

老闆 Vendor：歡迎，要內用還是外帶？

(Welcome, for here or to go?)

 

Tiffany: 嗨老闆！我們要外帶。請給我四個刈包，謝謝。

(Hi boss, we’d like to take it away, please give us four Gua Bao, thanks.)

 

老闆 Vendor：要加香菜嗎？ (With coriander?)

 

Amber: 兩個加兩個不要，花生粉可以多一點嗎，謝謝

(Two with coriander, the others without coriander, and can I have more peanut powder, thanks)

 

老闆走向A，並給A餐點

(The boss walked towards A, and passed the food to A)

 

老闆 Vendor：哈哈好，這是你的刈包，一共260元，謝謝。(Haha, ok, there you go, $260 in total, thanks)

 

Amber：300塊給你，謝謝。

(Here are $300 for you, thanks)

 

老闆 Vendor：40元找你，謝謝你。

(Here’s $40 change, thank you,)

 

Eric：下一個我們去買臭豆腐吧！

(Let’s get stinky tofu next!)

 

Manon：老闆，一個臭豆腐，內用！

(Boss, one stinky tofu, dine-in, please)

 

老闆 Vendor：好，馬上來！要什麼辣？

(Ok, it’ll be ready soon! What spicy level?)

 

Manon：小辣，謝謝 (Mild level spicy, thanks)

 

老闆 Vendor：來了！你的臭豆腐，總共60元。

(Here’s your stinky tofu, it’s $60 in total.)

 

Manon：這裡剛好，謝謝老闆。

(Here’s the exact change, thank you, boss)

 

老闆 Vendor：謝謝！ (Thank you)

劇終 THE END
